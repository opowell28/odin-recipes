No-Yeast Bread

No-yeast bread in a pinch.

Out of yeast? Out of time? This bread is quick and doesnt require yeast.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg

Steps

  2. Add wet ingredients and mix until a dough forms.
