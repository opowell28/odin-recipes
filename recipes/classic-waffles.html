Quick and Easy Waffles

These are the waffles that you think of when you think of waffles.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter, and vanilla.
  4. Pour the liquid mixture into the dry mixture and beat until blended and smooth.
  5. Pour batter onto the preheated waffle iron and cook until golden brown.
